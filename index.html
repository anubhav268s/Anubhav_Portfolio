Foresight by NavikTech

Live on the Google Play Store | www.naviktech.com

Designed and engineered as a smart driving assistant, Foresight evaluates GPS, weather, traffic, light conditions, and road types using a proprietary Weighted Multi-Factor Risk Model. It provides instant proactive warnings and safety feedback entirely on-device, ensuring zero server latency and maximum data privacy.

  • 100% Privacy-First Architecture: Zero-knowledge architecture with no centralized tracking servers. All calculations are local with a strict 7-day auto-delete policy.
  • Real-Time Sensor Fusion Engine: Synthesizes data from multiple sensors to compute dynamic risk probabilities and instantly penalizes erratic driving behavior.
  • Offline-First & Smart Throttling: Seamlessly functions in dead zones via "Smart Caching." Utilizes activity recognition to pause API polling and preserve battery life.
  • Safety Certificates: Generates verifiable "Certificates of Safety" (Grades A+ to F) and exportable CSV reports for insurance and RTO verification.
  • Media Coverage: Featured prominently in physical and e-paper news publications for inventing an innovative solution to prevent road accidents.
